## About burnedsignal

burnedsignal is a cybersecurity and intelligence blog dedicated to exposing what was meant to stay hidden. We analyze threats, investigate breaches, and share knowledge about digital security.

### Our Mission

In an era where digital threats evolve faster than defenses, we aim to:

- **Educate** - Share actionable intelligence and analysis
- **Expose** - Uncover vulnerabilities and threat actors
- **Empower** - Help organizations and individuals protect themselves

### What We Cover

- Threat intelligence and APT analysis
- Vulnerability research and disclosure
- OSINT techniques and methodologies
- Digital forensics and incident response
- Privacy and operational security
- Emerging technologies and their security implications
